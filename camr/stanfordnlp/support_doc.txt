<P>
The group chose to align themselves with the Convocation of
Anglicans in North America, CANA, a mission of the 17-million-
member Nigerian Anglican Church.
</P>
<P>
The Diocese of Nigeria, the largest, after Great Britain, of
Anglican Communion's 38 provinces, holds itself out as a safe
harbor for conservative congregations estranged from North
American dioceses.
</P>
<P>
CANA, founded in 2005, now includes 37 churches in 15 states and
the District of Columbia.
</P>
<P>
Another African group -- the 7-year-old Anglican Mission in the
Americas -- with oversight from Rwanda's Anglican province,
claims affiliation with 116 congregations in North America,
including 13 in Colorado, from Cortez to Broomfield.
</P>
<P>
Altogether, at least 200 of 7,200 Episcopal congregations in
North America have aligned themselves with overseas provinces.
</P>
